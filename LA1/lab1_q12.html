Emergency Helpline Numbers in India By Lakshya Mehrotra 24bci0212

Police Emergency
Provides immediate assistance for crime and safety issues.
Helpline: 100
Email: police@india.gov.in

Medical Emergency (Ambulance)
Offers emergency medical transport and health support.
Helpline: 108
Email: ambulance@india.gov.in

Fire and Rescue Services
Helps during fire accidents and rescue operations.
Helpline: 101
Email: fire@india.gov.in

Women Safety Helpline
Support for women facing harassment or violence.
Helpline: 181
Email: womenhelp@india.gov.in

Child Helpline
Assistance for children in distress.
Helpline: 1098
Email: childhelp@india.gov.in

Disaster Management
Provides support during natural disasters.
Helpline: 1078
Email: disaster@india.gov.in

Road Accident Emergency
Immediate help for road accidents.
Helpline: 1073
Email: roadsafety@india.gov.in

Railway Helpline
Help for railway-related emergencies.
Helpline: 139
Email: railway@india.gov.in

Cyber Crime Helpline
Report online fraud and cyber issues.
Helpline: 1930
Email: cybercrime@india.gov.in

Senior Citizen Helpline
Assistance for senior citizens.
Helpline: 14567
Email: seniorhelp@india.gov.in
